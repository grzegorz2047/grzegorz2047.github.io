Gem finder

This website allows you to find little gems in trending articles. Formula is simple. Gem is when 10$ post is voted by less then 100 people. You can manipulate ratio to change reward to vote ratio. If you change ratio or number of posts to check, it will automatically refresh list.

Type number of posts to check from 1 to 100
Type ratio from 0.1 to 1.5, to see what post is Valueable but without many votes
